02 — Concept

A new era of
development

Vibe Coding marks a revolution: the liberation of creativity. By delegating syntactic complexity to AI, participants break free from technical barriers to focus on what truly matters — solution architecture and real-world value.

100 students from Engineering, Computer Science, Business Administration, and Finance form pluridisciplinary teams to build AI-powered solutions for the Sahara region — guided by 15 professors and industry professionals throughout the full 48 hours.

Cursor GPT-4 Claude Gemini

Freedom to Innovate

Prototype without limits. Turn the impossible into reality using generative AI.

Direct Regional Impact

Water scarcity, economic development, and sustainable tourism — real challenges, real solutions.

Transformation Potential

Positioning Universiapolis as the crossroads of tech and financial innovation in Laâyoune — and beyond.
