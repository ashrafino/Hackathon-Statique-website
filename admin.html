Email & announcements (deliverability tips)

Resend (this site): In Netlify → Site settings → Environment variables, set RESEND_API_KEY and RESEND_FROM (example: Vibe Coding <noreply@yourdomain.com>). The address must be allowed in your Resend dashboard (verify your domain). Then use Send email (Resend) in the toolbar.

Free or low-cost sending: Use a proper transactional provider instead of PHP mail() or blind SMTP — try Resend, Brevo (Sendinblue), Mailgun, or Amazon SES (very cheap at scale). Many offer free monthly quotas for transactional mail (confirmations, password resets).

Newsletters / broadcasts: Use Brevo, Mailchimp, or Buttondown for opt-in lists — they handle unsubscribe links and compliance. Copying hundreds of addresses into BCC from this admin works for tiny tests but is risky for spam filters and privacy.

Avoid spam / Promotions: Send from a domain you control (not a free personal address as the official “From”). Add SPF, DKIM, and DMARC in DNS (your provider gives exact records). Keep a consistent sender name, use clear subject lines, avoid URL shorteners and spammy words, and only mail people who signed up. Warm up new domains gradually.

This dashboard does not send email by itself — export CSV/JSON or use “Copy BCC” for mail merge in your provider.
